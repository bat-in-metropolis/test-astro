Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A basic website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex applications can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing based on project requirements. After understanding your needs, we provide a detailed proposal outlining costs and deliverables. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! We offer various maintenance packages to keep your website or application running smoothly. This includes updates, security patches, content changes, and technical support. We're here for you long after launch.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a wide range of modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vue, Node.js, Python, and various CMS platforms. We select the best technology stack for each project based on your specific requirements.

Can you help with existing projects?

Absolutely! We can take over existing projects, whether you need enhancements, bug fixes, or a complete redesign. We'll start with a thorough audit to understand your current setup and recommend the best path forward.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, we work with clients globally. We use modern collaboration tools and maintain clear communication throughout the project. Many of our successful projects have been completed entirely remotely.

What information do you need to get started?

To begin, we need to understand your goals, target audience, budget, and timeline. Any existing brand materials, content, or competitor examples are also helpful. We'll guide you through the discovery process during our initial consultation.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all our websites are fully responsive and optimized for all devices. Mobile-first design is a core part of our development process, ensuring your site looks and performs beautifully on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Can you help with content creation?

Yes, we offer content creation services including copywriting, photography, and video production. We can work with your existing content or create everything from scratch to ensure your message resonates with your audience.
